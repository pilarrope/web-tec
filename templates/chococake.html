Chocolate cake


Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate powder
  • Chocolate cream
  • Forest fruits

Instructions:

Separate the whites from the yolks. Using the butterfly attachment, mount the whites until they form strong peaks. Reserve them in another bowl.

Incorporate the yolks together with the sweetener and vanilla in the robot bowl and beat well until they foam.

Add the oil and milk and combine well. Incorporate the solid ingredients, sieved and mix. Finally, integrate the egg whites, avoiding that they are lowered as little as possible. Pour into a mold with a hole in the center and bake 35/40 'at 165 Â° C. Take it out when poking the center with a toothpick comes out clean.

Let cool, unmold on a plate and decorate with melted chocolate and red berries.

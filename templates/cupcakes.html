Cupcakes


Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • lemon
  • Eggs
  • Strawberries
  • Cream

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 180ยบ. Arrange the paper capsules in a cupcake mold and reserve.

In a large bowl, pour the flour, baking soda and salt. Stir and sift.

Add the vanilla, oil and water and stir until all the ingredients are well integrated.

Divide the dough into the reserved capsules, filling 3/4 full.

Bake for approximately 20 minutes, or until a needle pokes the center and comes out clean.

Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely to room temperature.

Let cool at room temperature for 30 minutes and, once cold, beat with a few rods to emulsify so that the glaze takes in air. Put inside a pastry bag with a star tip and decorate each cupcake.

Sprinkle some salt flakes and serve.

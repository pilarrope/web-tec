Noodles


Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Lemon
  • Chicken
  • Soya sauce

Instructions:

First, put a saucepan with plenty of water with a little salt. As soon as it reaches a boil, pour the rice noodles into the water, let it rest for 4 minutes, strain and soak with cold water to stop cooking. Reserve.

In a separate frying pan, toast the almonds for a few minutes so that they brown on both sides. Remove and reserve.

Cut the onion and the two peppers into thin strips and, in the same pan and with a little oil, sauté for a few minutes over medium heat. The vegetables have to be a little crisp.

Clean and laminate the mushrooms and sauté them in the same way as the vegetables. Set aside with the rest of the already cooked food.

Cook the peas in boiling water until cooked, strain and add the mushrooms and vegetables.

Finally, cut the chicken breast into thin strips and put in the same pan over high heat. Cook on both sides until done and lightly toasted.

In a wok over medium-high heat, put a little oil. Add the cooked noodles, vegetables, mushrooms, peas, almonds, and chicken. Sauté a few minutes.

Pour in the soy sauce and stir well to combine the flavors. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and spread over two plates. Serve immediately.

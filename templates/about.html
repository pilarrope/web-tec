Hello! I'm Pilar


I study web design and development. But a change of direction in my personal life made me enter the world of cooking. And that's how Let's cooking with Pilar was born. The space where I put my soul and a hobby that fascinates me and fills me with life.

I'm passionate about photography, music, and I can't resist cheese. I am attracted to everything homemade, totally personalized and that hides a story.

I like to read, to be impregnated with new concepts, with ideas to carry out.

Every little detail inspires me, every word received, every criticism, the smell of what I'm cooking and that reminds me of all those recipes that I have yet to discover.

Self-taught in the kitchen, constantly searching for new flavors, unknown recipes, ingredients and challenges that push me to learn and improve every day.

Come on, let's cook!

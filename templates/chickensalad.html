Chicken salad


Ingredients:

  • Green beans
  • lemon
  • Chicken diced
  • Boiled eggs
  • Chicken diced
  • Quinoa
  • Tomatoes

Instructions:

For the salad, wash the quinoa well under the tap. Put it in a saucepan with the water and cook for 13 minutes, until it has absorbed the liquid. Now, let it rest for 5 minutes while you cut the rest of the ingredients.

Put all the food in a bowl with the quinoa and prepare the sauce.

For the pesto, blend all the ingredients together in a hand mixer, until a thick cream is left, and serve on top of the salad.

If you are not going to consume it immediately, keep both preparations in the fridge and add the sauce before serving.

Paella


Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Green beans

Instructions:

We start by chopping the leek very finely. Cut the green and driest part of the leek and discard it. Do the same with the root. Wash the rest of the leek with water to remove any dirt particles that remain between the leaves. Cut the leek into very thin slices.

Cut the chicken breast into cubes and reserve.

Drain the roasted peppers a little and cut them into strips or cubes. If you have time, you can roast pepper at home the day before and leave it ready to add to your quick paella.

Put the leek to poach in a pan. Add the chicken and brown everything together for a few minutes. Add the pepper, mix well and cook for 3 or 4 more minutes. Add the peas, rice and mix.

Add the broth, prawns and saffron. Raise the was until it comes to a boil and then lower it to medium low heat and let everything cook for 20 minutes. Remove from heat and let rest 5 minutes before serving.

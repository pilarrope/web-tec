Special toast


Ingredients:

  • Sliced bread
  • Egg
  • Pulled pork
  • Cheese
  • hollandaise sauce

Instructions:

Toast the slices of bread in the toaster or on the grill.

Cut the avocado in half, remove the skin and the seed and, with the help of a peeler, make thin strips that we will roll up on themselves, forming spirals.

Place the avocado rolls on each toast, distribute the pulled pork and top with the mozzarella and Camembert. Sprinkle a little black pepper on the surface of each toast and bake in a preheated oven on full power until the cheese is melted.

Remove, drizzle with a little hollandaise sauce, and serve the toast with wild flowers, avocado, and cheese immediately.
